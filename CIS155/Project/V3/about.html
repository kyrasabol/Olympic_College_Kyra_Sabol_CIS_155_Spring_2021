About the Author

Hi, my name is Kyra. I love the planet Earth, and I am lazy. I had gotten used to the three values of cheap, easy, and fast, but the more I learned about how my actions can have an affect on other people and our environment, I wanted to make some changes. It was hard (and still is!) to make changes, but the more gradually I do so, the better I feel about myself. I want to help others incorporate eco-friendly habits into their lifestyle with the idea that eco-friendly can be cheaper, easier, and faster than they think.

V2 (Week of: May 2-7)
This week I created the shopping pages and the recycle page. On the home page, I added a box shadow for my ready section. The shopping pages featured use of floats and background images. I don't care for the gradient or the borders, so that won't be a part of the next version. I'm not 100% comfortable with using a grid yet, so I haven't decided if my following versions will feature a grid. We'll see!
