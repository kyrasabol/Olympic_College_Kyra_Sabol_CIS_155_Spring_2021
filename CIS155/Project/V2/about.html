V1 (Week of: April 18-24)
This week I created the home page for my site. This included adding my navigation, adding sections such as my introduction, the tips section, and the about me section. I also created a basic style sheet for my home page. The CSS render the page in the font I wanted, created colored boxes to seperate my sections, and made the hyperlinks to internal and external sources much more aesthetically pleasing. In the future, I do think I will rearrange some of the CSS organization I have going on to make it easier to search and to apply rules. This first draft is a little scattered. I later hope to switch the "About Me" section into a side column and add a personal photo. I also might mess around with the look of the "Ready" section. The pages with "Under Construction" will see some added content over the next few weeks.

V2 (Week of: May 2-7)
This week I created the shopping pages and the recycle page. On the home page, I added a box shadow for my ready section. The shopping pages featured use of floats and background images. The recycle page featured use of borders, grids, and gradients. Formatting the grid was the most difficult part, but it actually looks really nice. I don't care for the gradient or the borders, so that won't be a part of the next version. I'm not 100% comfortable with using a grid yet, so I haven't decided if my following versions will feature a grid. We'll see!
